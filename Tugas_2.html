About Us

Le Túsa, it is irish for "With you. Our name echoes the unity we strive for. Just as Ireland represents a European gem, we embody the grace of European aesthetics intertwined with Indonesian zest. Le Túsa becomes a vessel for the convergence of tastes, a modern interpretation of a family's embrace. As crew members, we extend that familial warmth to our cherished guests.
"Where Tradition Meets Modernity". Every dish we present tells a story, a story of togetherness and unity. Whether it's the familiar taste of the West or the alluring flavors of Indonesia, each dish is a chapter in our journey. Our concept thrives no only on the plates but also in the connections forged around them.

We are avalaible everyday 7 AM - 12 PM.
Enjoy your day and see you in Le Túsa!
